★ Common questions

FAQs

What exactly do I receive after generating my hero? +
You receive a print-ready STL file — the universal format for 3D printing. This file works with all major FDM printers (Bambu Lab, Creality Ender, Prusa) and resin printers (Elegoo Saturn, Anycubic Photon). You can also send it to any local makerspace, print shop, or online service like Shapeways or JLCPCB.
Do you ship physical figures? +
Physical printing and shipping is currently in private beta. A limited number of customers are testing it now. Join our waitlist to be notified when beta opens in your region. All current orders receive a digital STL file for self-printing.
Is my child's photo safe? +
Completely. Your photo is processed in real time by our AI and permanently deleted immediately after the 3D model is generated. We never store, share, sell, or train AI models on uploaded photos. We comply with COPPA and GDPR guidelines for child data protection.
What photo gives the best result? +
A clear, front-facing photo taken in good natural light works best. The child should be the main subject — avoid busy backgrounds. A recent smartphone photo is perfectly fine. The better the photo, the more accurately the AI can capture your child's likeness.
I don't own a 3D printer. Can I still use 3DGENI? +
Absolutely. Once you download your STL file, you have several options: send it to a local library or makerspace (many offer free or low-cost printing), use an online print service like Shapeways, Craftcloud, or JLCPCB, or find a local 3D print shop. We're also building a print-and-ship service — join the waitlist to be first in line.
Are the superhero characters licensed from Marvel or DC? +
No — all 3DGENI hero archetypes are 100% original characters created and owned by 3DGENI. This means no IP conflicts and no licensing fees passed onto you. Your child becomes a unique, one-of-a-kind hero — not a copy of someone else's character.
